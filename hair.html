Hair Care:


Chequers hairstylists are advanced in precision cutting and the newest hair styles whether fashion-forward or traditional. We perform all types of permanent waves (perms) and curl relaxing and Keratin smoothing using a variety of techniques and state-of-the-art products to achieve each individual clients' desired look.

We also offer coloring, highlighting, balayage, and ombre using either traditional (low ammonia) or organic (no ammonia) hair color that covers grey hair beautifully and does not fade when Lanza colorcare or other high quality color shampoo is used.

 

L'anza shampoo, conditioners, and syling products are our salon favorites. The protein molecules used in L'anza shampoos and conditioners are the perfect size to penetrate the hair shaft and provide conditioning and repair that you can actually feel. Other proteins just sit on the outside and feel nice for a minute, never actually repairing damage from chemical services, heat styling, and environmental stresses. L'anza products contain moisturizing, smoothing Hawaiian botanical extracts (tamanu creme and kukui nut) and a patented "flower shield complex' that is part of their Healing Color Care product line. L'anza always uses the highest quality, sulfate-free ingredients.



L'anza Smoother is our favorite smoothing balm. It dramatically reduces drying time by blocking humidity--leaving your hair feeling soft and healthy. The fragrance alone will inspire you to take it home!
